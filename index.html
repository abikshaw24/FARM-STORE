ORGANIC FOOD FAQ

What does "organic" mean?

Organic products are grown in environmentally friendly ways. Toxic pesticides and agricultural chemicals are prohibited. Organic farming also conserves soil and water while using renewable resources.

Why eat organic?

Organic food contains fewer chemical residues and supports sustainable agriculture while protecting the environment.

Is organic food healthier?

Organic foods often contain higher antioxidants and are produced without synthetic pesticides and fertilizers.
